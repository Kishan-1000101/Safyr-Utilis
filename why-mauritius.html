A premier destination for business and investment in Africa

Leading Performance

The country is amongst the best-performing in Africa across a range of indices, including business, regulatory and macroeconomic environment and quality of living.

Modern Infrastructure

Mauritius also enjoys state-of-the-art infrastructure and communications networks, a diversified banking system, as well as a skilled, professional and bilingual workforce fluent in both English and French.

International Partnerships

In addition to being part of African Regional Economic Communities such as the Southern African Development Community (SADC) and the Common Market for Eastern and Southern Africa (COMESA), Mauritius is a signatory to a number of Double Taxation Avoidance Agreements and Investment Protection and Promotion Agreements.

Business Advantages

Located at the crossroads of Africa and Asia, Mauritius has enjoyed social and political stability since its independence in 1968. The country is amongst the best performing in Africa across a range of indices, including business, regulatory and macroeconomic environment, and quality of living.

Strategic Location

Mauritius serves as an ideal gateway for investments into Africa, India, and Asia, offering businesses a strategic location for regional headquarters and operations.

Legal Framework

A hybrid legal system combining English Common Law and French Civil Law, providing a comprehensive and flexible framework for businesses.

Economic Stability

Strong economic fundamentals with steady growth, low inflation, and a stable currency, creating a secure environment for business operations.

International Treaties

Extensive network of double taxation avoidance agreements (DTAAs) and investment promotion and protection agreements (IPPAs) with countries worldwide.

Favorable Tax Regime

Competitive tax rates, no capital gains tax, no withholding tax on dividends, and various incentives for businesses, making it an attractive investment destination.

Business-Friendly Environment

Streamlined processes for business registration, no exchange controls, and protection of intellectual property rights, facilitating ease of doing business.

Work and Live in Mauritius

Mauritius is more than just a tropical island; it is a thriving hub for high-quality living, business and investment. Surrounded by turquoise lagoons and lush green landscapes, the country also boasts excellent infrastructure, political stability, and a welcoming community. Its multicultural heritage is reflected in everything from architecture and cuisine to the island's rich calendar of festivals, creating a vibrant lifestyle where the warmth of tradition is seamlessly woven into modern living. This rich cultural fabric also informs business practices, fostering an environment where innovation and tradition coexist harmoniously. For those looking to relocate, Mauritius offers a variety of residence pathways, including the Occupation Permit, Residence Permit and Permanent Residence Permit, as well as other attractive options such as the Property Development Scheme, Premium Visa/Digital Nomad Visa and Family Occupation Permit - making it easy to find the perfect fit for your lifestyle and ambitions.

Quality of Life

Enjoy a high standard of living with pristine beaches, year-round tropical climate, modern healthcare facilities, and international schools.

Work Permits

Streamlined processes for obtaining work permits for qualified professionals, with Safyr Utilis providing comprehensive support throughout the application process.

Residence Options

Multiple residence programs available, including the Premium Visa, Occupation Permit, and Residence Permit for retirees, offering flexibility based on individual needs.

Modern Infrastructure

State-of-the-art infrastructure and communications networks, reliable utilities, and excellent connectivity to major global destinations.
