Corporate Secretarial Services

Corporate Secretarial Services

Our corporate secretarial services ensure that your business meets all regulatory requirements while maintaining good corporate governance practices. We handle the administrative burden so you can focus on growing your business.

With our extensive experience in corporate services, we provide comprehensive support to help your business thrive in today's competitive environment.

Our Corporate Services Include:

Corporate Secretarial Services

  • Company secretarial services
  • Board meetings and Annual General Meeting support, including preparation and circulation of board packs
  • Compliance with licensing conditions and filing requirements
  • Arrange execution of agreements and documents in consultation with clients and/or lawyers

Corporate Finance Services via Safyr Capital Partners

Via Safyr Capital Partners, we can assist you with the following:

  • Corporate finance assignments
  • Trade and treasury support
  • Assistance on banking and investment solutions
  • Ancillary administrative services and office facilities support

Legal Services via SC Legal

Through our partnership with SC Legal Ltd, a reputable Mauritian law firm specialising in commercial law, we assist in meeting all your legal needs in terms of advice and ongoing support.

Fiduciary Services

  • Advice and assistance on setting up a trust or foundation
  • Fiduciary services such as trusteeship
  • Assistance with administering a foundation
  • Any associated tax or regulatory compliance

Expatriation Solutions

  • Liaison with the Economic Development Board and the Passport and Immigration Office
  • Assistance with work and residence permit applications

Consultation

  • Advice on setting up structures in Mauritius
  • Advice on and assistance with opening up bank accounts in Mauritius and other selected jurisdictions

Set-up

  • Company incorporation in Mauritius and other selected jurisdictions
  • Assistance with incorporation of other vehicles such as a protected cell company or société
  • Provision of registered office services
  • Assistance with special licences

Directorship Services

  • Provision of local directors

Administrative and Management Services

  • Financial accounting, reporting and consolidation of accounts
  • Regulatory and tax compliance
  • Bank account administration
  • Payroll services
