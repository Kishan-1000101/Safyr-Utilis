Your Fund Administration Journey

Experience a seamless progression through our comprehensive suite of fund administration services, each designed to optimize your fund's performance, ensure regulatory compliance and enhance stakeholder value.

01

Strategic Consultation & Planning

Begin your journey with expert guidance on fund structure optimization and regulatory pathway planning in Mauritius consisting:

  • Comprehensive fund structure analysis and recommendations
  • Regulatory pathway mapping and compliance planning
  • Establishment of banking relationships across jurisdictions
  • Risk assessment and mitigation strategy development
02

Fund Launch & Setup Excellence

We provide seamless fund establishment with comprehensive onboarding, licensing support and operational framework implementation, including:

  • End-to-end fund setup services and investor onboarding
  • Special licence acquisition support (CIS, Asset Manager, Investment Dealer, Investment Advisers, etc.)
  • Registered office services and corporate governance setup
  • Capital call assistance and initial funding coordination
  • Professional directorship services and board support
03

Operational Excellence & Administration

We deliver comprehensive daily fund operations with precision-driven accounting, valuation and investor communication services namely:

  • Daily fund and portfolio accounting with real-time reporting
  • Accurate NAV calculations and investor distribution processing
  • Comprehensive back-office and middle-office support
  • Professional investor relations and communication management
  • Treasury operations and cash management solutions
04

Ongoing Management & Compliance

We are committed to providing continuous fund management support with proactive compliance monitoring and strategic corporate secretarial services, namely:

  • Ongoing corporate compliance monitoring and governance management.
  • Professional corporate secretarial services
  • Regulatory reporting and submission coordination
  • Ongoing strategic advisory and optimization recommendations
  • Performance monitoring and enhancement strategies
