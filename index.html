Welcome to Safyr Utilis

Safyr Utilis is part of a financial services group based in Mauritius, combining wealth structuring and fund administration with an investment banking reach.

Established following the merger of Safyr Wealth Services Ltd and Utilis Corporate Services Ltd, Safyr Utilis offers specialist services in wealth structuring, including international companies and trusts, corporate services, tax, accounting and regulatory services, fund administration and business solutions such as work and residency permits.

Newsroom Why Mauritius?

Located at the crossroads of Africa and Asia, Mauritius has enjoyed social and political stability since its independence in 1968. The country is amongst the best performing in Africa across a range of indices, including business, regulatory and macroeconomic environment, and quality of living.

Mauritius also enjoys state-of-the-art infrastructure and communications networks, as well as a skilled, professional bilingual (English and French) workforce.

Newsroom Business Advantages

Expanding offshore business and capabilities is seamless with Mauritius' wide array of double taxation avoidance agreements.

Create or expand your existing business on a global level with no exchange controls in a business-friendly environment.

Empower investors to retain earnings in Mauritius under favorable tax rates and safeguard against political and economic risks.

Newsroom Quality of Life

Mauritius offers an exceptional quality of life with its pristine beaches, year-round tropical climate, and multicultural society.

The island boasts world-class healthcare facilities, international schools, and a safe living environment for expatriates and their families.

Mauritius' residence programs offer attractive options for business professionals and retirees looking to relocate.
