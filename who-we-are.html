About Safyr Utilis

Safyr Utilis is part of a financial services group based in Mauritius, combining wealth structuring and fund administration with an investment banking reach.

Established following the merger of Safyr Wealth Services Ltd and Utilis Corporate Services Ltd, Safyr Utilis offers specialist services in wealth structuring, including international companies and trusts, corporate services, tax, accounting and regulatory services, fund administration and business solutions such as work, and residency permits.
