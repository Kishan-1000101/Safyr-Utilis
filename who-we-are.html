About Safyr Utilis

Established in 2016, Safyr Utilis is a licenced corporate service provider, which forms part of the Safyr group in Mauritius. Its expertise spans corporate and fund administration, wealth structuring, and capital markets, investment banking solutions, offering tailored solutions for a diverse clientele. Safyr Utilis serves African, US, Asian, Middle East and European markets, leveraging deep industry knowledge and strategic partnerships.

Its dedicated team, comprising seasoned professionals and in-house experts like lawyers, accountants, and tax advisors, is committed to delivering superior client service. Whether navigating complex fund strategies or safeguarding wealth, Safyr Utilis ensures excellence in every aspect of financial management. Safyr Utilis has its headquarters in Mauritius with offices in Seychelles and South Africa and operates internationally.
